## What is The Craft of Change?

The Craft of Change is for people who see themselves as craftsmen of change inside established organisations — leaders who use technology not as an end in itself, but as a lever for transformation.

They're not founders. They're not waiting for permission to act. They're the people inside large organisations who make things happen anyway — translating between the world of technology and the world of business, navigating politics, building coalitions, and pushing organisations forward even when it's hard.

This newsletter is built around a simple belief: that transformation is a craft, not an event. And like any craft, it improves with study, practice, and honest feedback from people who've been there.

## About the author

The Craft of Change is written by Ivan Rapin-Smith. I've spent 30 years building at the intersection of technology, organisations, and change — co-founding startups, running accelerators on two continents, and since 2014 leading Watsco Ventures, the corporate venture and startup studio of a Fortune 500 company.

I write about what history tells us about the present — because the patterns repeat, and understanding them is one of the few genuine edges available to anyone navigating transformation today.
